## Competitor Gaps
Existing content on outside counsel guidelines falls into two camps: (1) generic "what are OCGs" explainers that list standard provisions without addressing the 2025-2026 tightening trend, and (2) vendor-specific pieces from e-billing companies (Brightflag, Aderant) that focus narrowly on billing compliance without covering AI provisions, staffing requirements, or the strategic response firms need. Nobody is connecting the dots between stricter guidelines and the operational changes firms must make, particularly around AI adoption, knowledge management, and workflow integration. The Epiq and Williams Lea pieces touch on AI in OCGs but from the corporate side, not the law firm adaptation angle.
