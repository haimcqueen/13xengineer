# Legora - Product Knowledge

## What Legora Is

Legora is a collaborative AI workspace for lawyers. It helps legal teams spend less time managing process and more time delivering value to clients. The platform integrates directly into existing legal workflows (Word, iManage, SharePoint, legal databases) so adoption is frictionless.

**Website**: https://legora.ai
**Positioning**: "Smarter tools, better outcomes"
**CEO**: Max Junestrand

## Products

### Tabular Review
- Analyze thousands of documents at scale
- AI suggests markup and flags issues
- Replaces manual document review that typically takes weeks
- Designed for due diligence, contract review, and regulatory analysis

### Word Add-in
- Lives inside Microsoft Word where lawyers already work
- Draft and rewrite clauses using the firm's own precedent library
- Context-aware: understands the document being edited
- No copy-pasting between tools; everything happens inline

### Legal Research
- Access legal databases, DMS, iManage, and SharePoint from one interface
- AI-powered search across internal and external knowledge
- Finds relevant precedent, case law, and firm work product
- Reduces research time from hours to minutes

## Differentiators

1. **Built for collaboration**: Not a single-user chatbot. Teams share context, precedent, and workflows.
2. **Integrates where lawyers work**: Word, iManage, SharePoint. No new tabs or separate apps.
3. **Firm-specific intelligence**: Uses the firm's own precedent and work product, not generic templates.
4. **Enterprise-grade security**: Data isolation, SSO, audit trails. Meets law firm compliance requirements.
5. **Adoption that sticks**: 80% active usage at BAHR proves lawyers actually use it (most legal AI tools see <20% adoption).
